New York State Travel Guide

From the bright lights of Manhattan to the thundering Niagara Falls, New York State offers an incredible diversity of experiences. The Empire State features bustling cities, pristine wilderness in the Adirondacks, world-class wineries in the Finger Lakes, and historic landmarks throughout the Hudson Valley.

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Amtrak connects major cities across the state. For exploring regions like the Adirondacks or Finger Lakes, renting a car is recommended. Major airports serve NYC, Buffalo, Albany, and Rochester.

Best Time to Visit

Fall offers spectacular foliage and comfortable temperatures. Summer is perfect for outdoor activities. Winter brings excellent skiing, while spring showcases beautiful gardens and waterfalls.

Regional Highlights

Don't miss the Finger Lakes wine trails, Adirondack hiking, Niagara Falls boat tours, Hudson Valley historic sites, and the Thousand Islands region.
