Buffalo, New York

Welcome to Buffalo, a city reinventing itself through its rich architectural heritage, thriving food scene, and revitalized waterfront. As the gateway to Niagara Falls, Buffalo combines historic charm with modern attractions, offering visitors a unique blend of culture, cuisine, and natural wonder.

Travel Tips

Getting Around

Buffalo's Metro Rail connects downtown to North Buffalo. The NFTA provides bus service throughout the city. For Niagara Falls visits, consider the Discover Niagara Shuttle.

Best Time to Visit

Summer offers festivals and outdoor events. Fall brings beautiful colors and mild weather. Winter features snow activities and holiday markets. Spring showcases cherry blossoms at the Japanese Garden.

Local Events

Don't miss the Taste of Buffalo (July), Garden Walk (Summer), Buffalo Wing Festival (Labor Day weekend), and Christmas Market at Canalside (December).
