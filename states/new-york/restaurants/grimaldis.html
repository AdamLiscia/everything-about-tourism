Located under the Brooklyn Bridge, Grimaldi's is a New York institution known for its coal-fired brick oven pizzas. Using fresh ingredients and traditional Neapolitan techniques, each pizza is made to order in the iconic coal-fired oven that gives their pies their distinctive crisp and charred crust.

Visitor Tips

What to Know

Cash only. No reservations. No slices - whole pies only. No delivery. Worth the wait for the authentic coal-fired experience.

Best Times to Visit

Arrive before opening or during off-peak hours (2-5 PM) to avoid long lines. Weekday lunches are less crowded than weekends.

Local Tips

Take your pizza to Brooklyn Bridge Park for amazing views. Visit the ATM before arriving. Consider ordering a small pie first - you can always order more.
