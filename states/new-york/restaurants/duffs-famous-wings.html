Wings • American

Since 1969, Duff's has been serving Buffalo's favorite wings, known for their extra-hot sauce and the warning "Medium is Hot, Medium Hot is Very Hot, Hot is Very Very Hot!" Featured on numerous TV shows and visited by presidents, Duff's represents Buffalo wing culture at its finest.

Visitor Tips

Heat Levels

Start one level below what you normally order elsewhere. Their heat scale is serious! Light is mild, Medium is hot, Hot is extremely hot.

Best Times to Visit

Weekday lunch for shorter waits. Expect 30-45 minute waits during peak dinner hours and weekends. Call ahead seating available.

Local Tips

Try the blue cheese dressing. Wings are available for takeout. Multiple locations available but the Sheridan Drive spot is the original.
