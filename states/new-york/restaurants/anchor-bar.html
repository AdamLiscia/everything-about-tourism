Home of the original Buffalo wings, created by Teressa Bellissimo in 1964. This historic restaurant serves their famous wings alongside Italian-American classics in a casual, memorabilia-filled atmosphere that celebrates Buffalo's culinary heritage.

Visitor Tips

When to Visit

Lunch hours are less crowded. Expect longer waits during dinner and weekends. Call ahead seating available for parties of 6 or more.

What to Order

Try the original medium sauce first. Order blue cheese (not ranch!). Don't skip the celery. Consider splitting a double order for variety.

Local Tips

Visit the gift shop for wing sauce and merchandise. Take photos with the historic memorabilia. Free parking available in the adjacent lot.
