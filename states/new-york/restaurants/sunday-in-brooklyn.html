New American

A beloved Williamsburg destination known for its innovative take on modern American cuisine. Famous for their malted pancakes and seasonal menus, Sunday in Brooklyn offers a fresh, contemporary dining experience in a warm, inviting space.

Visitor Tips

Reservations

Highly recommended for brunch and dinner. Book at least 2 weeks in advance for weekend brunch. Walk-ins accepted but expect a wait.

Best Times to Visit

Weekday mornings are quietest. Weekend brunch is busiest. Dinner service is more relaxed. Bar area available for walk-ins.

Local Tips

Try the seasonal cocktails. Outdoor seating available in warmer months. Don't skip the house-made pastries. Great for groups.
