Canalside

Canalside is Buffalo's premier waterfront destination, offering year-round entertainment and activities. Located at the historic Erie Canal Harbor, this revitalized district features concerts, festivals, water activities, ice skating, and cultural attractions that celebrate Buffalo's rich maritime heritage.

Free concerts, outdoor fitness classes, water bikes, kayaking, and food festivals.

Visitor Information

Getting There

Located in downtown Buffalo. Paid parking available in nearby lots. Metro Rail stops at Erie Canal Harbor Station. Free NFTA Metro shuttle during events.

Best Times to Visit

Summer for concerts and water activities. Winter for ice skating and holiday events. Check event calendar for special programming.

Local Tips

Bring lawn chairs for concerts. Rent water bikes early in peak season. Visit on Thursdays for extended hours and special programming.
