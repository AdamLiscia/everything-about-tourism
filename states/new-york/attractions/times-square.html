Times Square

Known as "The Crossroads of the World," Times Square is the vibrant heart of Manhattan's Theater District. This iconic intersection, stretching from West 42nd to West 47th Streets, dazzles millions of visitors annually with its massive digital billboards, Broadway theaters, street performers, and endless entertainment options.

Must-See Attractions

TKTS Booth

Get discounted same-day tickets to Broadway shows. The iconic red steps offer great photo opportunities.

Show Times

Broadway Shows

Experience world-class theater at dozens of historic venues in the Theater District.

Current Shows

Madame Tussauds

Interactive wax museum featuring celebrity figures and immersive experiences.

Buy Tickets

Things to Do

Shopping

Explore flagship stores, souvenir shops, and major retail destinations like the Disney Store and M&M's World.

Dining

From casual eateries to themed restaurants like Hard Rock Cafe and Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.

Entertainment

Street performers, live music, and special events throughout the year, including the New Year's Eve celebration.

Visitor Information

Getting There

Multiple subway lines stop at Times Square-42nd St station. Walking distance from Port Authority Bus Terminal and Penn Station.

Best Times to Visit

Most dazzling at night. Less crowded in early morning. Expect large crowds during holidays and Broadway show times.

Safety Tips

Well-patrolled area. Be aware of your surroundings. Watch for pickpockets in crowds. Visit NYPD Times Square Substation if needed.
