Finger Lakes

Discover the pristine beauty of New York's Finger Lakes region, where 11 glacial lakes create a stunning landscape of rolling hills, cascading waterfalls, and award-winning vineyards. This region offers world-class wineries, outdoor adventures, and charming lakeside towns.

Must-Do Experiences

Seneca Lake Wine Trail

Explore over 30 wineries along the deepest of the Finger Lakes, known for exceptional Rieslings and stunning lake views.

Watkins Glen State Park

Hike through a dramatic gorge featuring 19 waterfalls along a stunning 2-mile trail.

Visitor Information

Best Time to Visit

Summer and fall are peak seasons. Summer offers perfect weather for water activities, while fall brings stunning foliage and grape harvest festivals.

Getting Around

A car is recommended to explore the region. Major airports nearby include Rochester, Syracuse, and Ithaca. Wine tour services available for tastings.

Local Tips

Book winery tours in advance during peak season. Consider staying in lakeside B&Bs. Don't miss the local farmers markets and craft breweries.
