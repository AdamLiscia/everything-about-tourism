Since 1837, Schwabl's has been a Buffalo institution, famous for their hand-carved beef on weck and traditional German-American cuisine. This historic restaurant maintains its old-world charm while serving some of Buffalo's most iconic dishes in a warm, family atmosphere.

Famous Dishes

Beef on Weck

Hand-carved roast beef on a kimmelweck roll, served with horseradish and au jus. Buffalo's signature sandwich.

$14.99

German Potato Salad

Traditional warm potato salad with bacon, vinegar, and herbs. A perfect side dish.

$4.99

Sauerbraten

Traditional German pot roast marinated in vinegar and spices, served with potato dumplings.

$24.99

Visitor Tips

What to Know

Cash only. No reservations. Traditional table service. Historic atmosphere with vintage decor. Watch your beef being hand-carved at the carving station.

Best Times to Visit

Lunch hours can be busy with locals. Early dinner is less crowded. Saturday evenings are busiest. Closed Mondays.

Local Tips

Try the horseradish - it's fresh and strong! Ask about daily specials. Free parking available. Consider the combo with German potato salad.
