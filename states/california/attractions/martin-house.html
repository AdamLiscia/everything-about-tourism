Martin House

The Martin House Complex is Frank Lloyd Wright's Prairie School masterpiece, designed and built between 1903-1905. This National Historic Landmark showcases Wright's revolutionary design principles through its integrated architecture, art glass, and furnishings, all meticulously restored to their original glory.

Getting There

Free parking available. Accessible by public transit (Metro Bus #11 or #25). Located in Buffalo's historic Parkside neighborhood.
