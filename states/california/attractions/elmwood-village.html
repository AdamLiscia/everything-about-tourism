Elmwood Village

Elmwood Village is one of Buffalo's most charming neighborhoods, known for its tree-lined streets, Victorian architecture, and vibrant commercial district. This walkable community features locally-owned boutiques, cafes, restaurants, and cultural attractions, making it a favorite destination for both locals and visitors.

Neighborhood Highlights

Shopping

Browse unique boutiques, antique shops, bookstores, and local artisan goods along Elmwood Avenue.

Visitor Information

Getting Around

Street parking available. Walkable neighborhood. NFTA Metro Bus routes 20 and 26 serve Elmwood Avenue. Bike-friendly with dedicated lanes.

Best Times to Visit

Summer for outdoor dining and events. Fall for beautiful foliage. Saturday farmers market (May-November). First Fridays feature gallery openings.

Local Events

Elmwood Avenue Festival of the Arts (August), Holiday Market (December), weekly farmers market, and various community events throughout the year.
