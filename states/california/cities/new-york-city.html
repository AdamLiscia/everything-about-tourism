Travel Tips

Getting Around

The NYC subway system runs 24/7 and is the most efficient way to travel. Consider getting a MetroCard for unlimited rides. For intercity travel, Amtrak serves major cities across the state.

Where to Stay

Manhattan offers luxury hotels and boutique accommodations, while Brooklyn and Queens provide more affordable options. Consider staying near subway stations for convenient access to attractions.

Local Events

Don't miss the NYC Marathon (November), New Year's Eve in Times Square, NYC Restaurant Week (January/July), and the Tribeca Film Festival (Spring).
