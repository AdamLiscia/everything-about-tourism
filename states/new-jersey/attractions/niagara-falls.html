Niagara Falls

Experience one of the world's most magnificent natural wonders. Niagara Falls consists of three waterfalls that straddle the border between New York and Ontario, Canada, creating an awe-inspiring display of nature's power and beauty.

Visitor Information

Best Time to Visit

Summer (June-August) offers warmest weather and full access to attractions. Spring and Fall provide smaller crowds and beautiful scenery. Winter features stunning ice formations.

Getting There

Located 30 minutes from Buffalo Airport. Free shuttle service available from major hotels. Parking available at Niagara Falls State Park ($10-20/day).

Essential Tips

Bring waterproof gear and wear sturdy shoes. Consider purchasing a Discovery Pass for multiple attractions. Visit early morning or evening for smaller crowds.
