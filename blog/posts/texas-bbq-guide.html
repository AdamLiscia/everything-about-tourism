Top 10 BBQ Joints in Texas You Can't Miss

By Mike Wilson March 12, 2024 8 min read

In Texas, BBQ isn't just food - it's a way of life. From smoky brisket to fall-off-the-bone ribs, these legendary BBQ joints serve up the best of Texas tradition.

1. Franklin Barbecue - Austin

What makes it special: Aaron Franklin's brisket has achieved legendary status, and for good reason. The perfectly rendered fat, the peppery bark, and the signature smoke ring make this some of the best BBQ in the world.

Wait Time: 3-5 hours

Must Try: Brisket, Espresso BBQ Sauce

Pro Tip: Get in line before 8am

Texas BBQ: Know Before You Go

  • Most places sell out early - plan accordingly
  • Brisket is king in Texas
  • Many spots are cash-only
  • Don't ask for sauce (seriously)

Texas BBQ Glossary

Bark

The flavorful, crusty exterior that forms on smoked meats

Smoke Ring

The pink layer just beneath the bark, indicating proper smoking
