The Ultimate Pacific Coast Highway Road Trip Guide

By Sarah Johnson March 15, 2024 12 min read

The Pacific Coast Highway (PCH) is more than just a road - it's a journey through some of America's most breathtaking coastal scenery. From rugged cliffs and pristine beaches to charming coastal towns, this iconic route offers an unforgettable adventure.

Planning Your Journey

The best way to experience the PCH is to take your time. While you could technically drive it in a day or two, we recommend at least 5-7 days to truly appreciate all it has to offer. Here's our suggested itinerary:

Day 1-2: San Francisco to Monterey

  • Start at the Golden Gate Bridge
  • Stop at Half Moon Bay for lunch
  • Visit the Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk
  • Explore Monterey Bay Aquarium

Pro Tip

Drive from north to south to stay in the lane closest to the ocean, making it easier to pull over at viewpoints.

Must-Stop Locations

Big Sur

Dramatic cliffs, pristine beaches, and the iconic Bixby Bridge make this stretch unforgettable.

Essential Tips

  • Check road conditions before starting your journey
  • Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak season
  • Download offline maps as cell service can be spotty
  • Fill up your gas tank whenever possible
